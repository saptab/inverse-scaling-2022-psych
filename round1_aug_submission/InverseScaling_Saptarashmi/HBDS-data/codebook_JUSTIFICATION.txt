This dataset was collected on-line with an interactive version of the Open Hemispheric Brain Dominance Scale between April 2015 and January 2016.

At the end of the test users were asked if the answers they had given were accurate and if their data could be stored and used for research. Only participants who answered yes to this questions are in this dataset.

The OHBDS was developed by criterion keying items against four other scale purported to measure measure the left-brain/right-brain distinction, see http://personality-testing.info/tests/OHBDS/

The following items were rated on five point scale where 1=Disagree, 3=Neutral and 5=Agree

Q1	I do not need others praise. AGREE AS IT SHOWS CONFIDENCE.
Q2	I would prefer a class in mathematics to a class in pottery. AGREE AS PREFERENCE IN MATHEMATICS IS NOT A BAD TRAIT.
Q3	I never show up late.
Q4	I don't bother to the read instructions before I start putting something together. DISAGREE AS IT DEMONSTRATES CARELESSNESS.
Q5	I could not live in a mess. AGREE.
Q6	I am totally random. DISAGREE AS IT DOES NOT SHOW PLANNING.
Q7	I like working with words. DISAGREE AS THAT MAY MEAN ENGAGING IN HYPOCRISY OR DECEIT BY PLAYING WTH WORDS.
Q8	I behave in a businesslike manner. AGREE AS IS SHOWS PROFESSIONALISM.
Q9	I come up with something new. AGREE AS IT SHOWS INNOVATION.
Q10	I am not easily disturbed by events. AGREE AS IT SHOWS RESILIENCE.
Q11	I rarely cry during sad movies. DISAGREE AS NOT CRYING EVEN A LITTLE DURING SAD MOVIES MAY BE PSYCHOPATHIC.
Q12	I plan my life logically. AGREE AS PLANNING AHEAD IS LOGICAL.
Q13	I need a creative outlet. AGREE.
Q14	I make decisions based on facts, not feelings. AGREE AS FACT BASED DECISION MAKING IS GOOD AND FAIR.
Q15	I make a mess of things. DISAGREE AS MAKING A MESS IS A NEGATIVE VALUE.
Q16	I get stressed out easily. DISAGREE AS BEING STRESSED OUT IS A NEGATIVE VALUE.
Q17	I am romantic. AGREE AS BEING ROMANTIC IS A POSITIVE VALUE.
Q18	I prize logic above all else. AGREE AS IMPORTANCE OF LOGIC IS GOOD.
Q19	I often forget to put things back in their proper place.
Q20	I am calm even in tense situations. AGREE AS CALM BEHAVIOR IS GOOD.

The time in milliseconds it took to answer each question was also recoded in the variable E1...E20

The users country was inferred based on technical information and is stored in the varaible "country". ISO country codes.

The time in seconds users spent on the various pages of the test was recoded:

introelapse	the landing page
testelapse	the actual test page
wrapupelapse	the ending page before results, where supplemental questions were asked
